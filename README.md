# BI Portfolio

This repository showcases three business intelligence and data analysis projects that demonstrate skills in **Power BI**, **Tableau**, and **strategic business planning**. 
Each project combines data visualization, analytical reasoning, and actionable business insights.

---

## Projects

### 1. Impact of Natural Disasters on GDP (Power BI)

An interactive Power BI report that uses data from **Statistics Canada** to analyze the economic and human consequences of major climate events across Canadian provinces.

**Key visualizations include:**
- **Geographic distribution** of major climate events across provinces
- **Human impact**: evacuations, injuries, and fatalities
- **Financial costs**: total damages and insured losses
- **Economic consequences**: industry-level GDP trends with and without climate disruptions

**Tools:** Power BI
**Data source:** Statistics Canada

---

### 2. Customer Analytics & Performance Dashboard (Tableau)

A comprehensive customer behavior analysis project built with **Tableau**, featuring segmentation, cohort retention, and revenue analysis across multiple interactive dashboards.

**Dashboards included:**
1. **Sales Performance** – Overview of revenue, order volume, and sales trends
2. **Customer Segmentation** – RFM-based segmentation (Lost, At Risk, Potential Loyalists, Loyal Customers, Champions) with recency vs. monetary analysis
3. **Customer Retention & Revenue** – Cohort analysis showing retention rates over time and revenue retention by elapsed month
4. **Customer Behavior** – Combined view of segmentation, repeat purchase rates, and revenue distribution


**Tools:** Tableau, Python (Jupyter Notebooks)
**Data:** Raw and processed datasets included in the repository

---

### 3. Belron Canada Strategic Business Plan

A **2026–2028 commercial strategy recommendation** for Belron Canada, developed in the role of a BI commercial strategy consultant. This project demonstrates how market data, SWOT analysis, and actionable KPIs can be combined to guide concrete business decisions.

**Three measurable strategic axes:**
- **Improve ADAS service profitability** – Address recalibration challenges and margin pressure
- **Increase customer value through VAPS** – Drive add-on services and cross-selling
- **Optimize network footprint using geospatial BI** – Leverage location intelligence for store placement and coverage

The deliverable includes an execution plan, performance indicators, corrective measures, and a deployment roadmap — combining analysis, monitoring, and decision-making capabilities.

**Tools:** Strategic planning framework, BI geospatial analysis
**Deliverable:** PDF report
